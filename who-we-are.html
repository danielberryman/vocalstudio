EPIC DUETS

Love, conflict, and harmony—told through two voices. Duets only!

Performed at The Green Room 42 in New York City.

Epic Duets is a heartfelt, hilarious, and harmony-filled celebration of musical theatre’s most intimate form: the duet. With songs ranging from the soaring intimacy of Sondheim’s Move On to the chaotic charm of Bad Idea from Waitress, these performances explore love, family, conflict, and connection through the dynamic interplay of two voices. Drawing from the full musical theatre canon, each duet invites audiences into the messy, magical experience of navigating partnership—onstage and off.
